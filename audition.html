Step 1: Prepare!

Prepare the audition material and warm ups with the audition packets! These contain everything you need to know to complete your audition process.

Step 2: Record!

Set up a camera and record where you are with the warm ups. Make sure to record the best work, and upload to YouTube or Google Drive; make sure that the video can be viewed!

Step 3: Apply!

Fill out the application through Google Forms to express your interest in auditioning for the ensemble.

Step 4: Play Live!

If selected, attend the live audition at Bridgeforth Stadium June 24th, 2023.

Step 5: Now... Wait!

Give the leadership team and staff time to go through all of the auditions and evaluate who will make the 2023 iteration of the Marching Royal Dukes Percussion Section.
