About MRD Percussion


The MRD Percussion section is a group of highly talented percussionists at James Madison University. As members of the Marching Royal Dukes, the MRD Percussion section are liasons for the musical excellence found at JMU. We serve the university by going to high school competitions to advertise the univeristy to high schoolers interested in going to the university.

James Madison University is one of few colleges/universities that have a full percussion section in the marching band. Most marching bands don't have a front ensemble, and some of them do not have a cymbal section in the drumline; the Marching Royal Dukes believe that anyone who wants to continue marching band in college should be free to do so, and in order to meet that belief, we provide several ways for percussionists to continue in band.

As one of two sections of the Marching Royal Dukes that need to be auditioned into, the ensemble is competitive and the best percussionists compose the group. The audition process for the ensemble drives members to keep practicing through the offseasons and make sure that the strongest drumline is created every season.
