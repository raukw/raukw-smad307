Frequently Asked Questions

How often are rehearsals?
The Marching Royal Dukes have sectional rehearsals on Mondays and full band rehearsals Tuesday through Thursday. If there is a performance that weekend, the band will rehearse Friday as well. Practices last about an hour and 45 minutes for the percussion section.

Is the audition process hard?
The audition process is meant to ensure that the best performers are on the line. No one is guaranteed a spot, so every year is competitive.

How do I know what section has more open spots?
Every spot, except for leadership, is open and up for grabs, whether it's a returner or freshman!

How long does the season last?
The season starts a week and a half before school, and will last through the football season. Some years, there are extra trips the band participates in, but that will vary year to year.

How do I audition?
The first round consists of a recording of the warm up packet found below. Callback auditions are held in person at Bridgeforth Stadium, and the drumline will be set following auditions.
